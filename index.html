Calculations

Dew point is shown in °F as a moisture and comfort signal. It is not a humidity percentage.

Apparent temperature is displayed from Weather.gov heat index or wind chill when available. Otherwise the app uses the same NWS heat index and wind chill formulas.

Estimated WBGT combines wet-bulb temperature, air temperature, wind, cloud cover, time of day, date, and sun angle. It is an approximation for running conditions, not an official measured WBGT.

Score is roughly 70% thermal comfort, 20% rain, and 10% wind. Heat, severe wind chill, and missing thermal data can cap or reduce the final score.
